What are Pendants
Pendants are also called as drop lights , suspended lights which are ambient stylish and offer a beautiful decor to the rooms. Pendants are usually hung by a single cord or by metal rod and in rare instances by a chain. Pendants are available in metal , acrylic and fabric. Pendants evidently can provide a good illumination when you need a combination of task lighting and ambient lighting. The narrower the shade the light from the pendant is highly focused. .
