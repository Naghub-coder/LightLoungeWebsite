What is Glare Free light?
Glare is difficulty in seeing things in the presence of a light. Imagine you are outside of a shade on a very hot and sunny day. The bright sunlight does not allow you to focus on any object clearly. So we wear dark eye glass shades to soften the bright sunlight and to absorb the glare In the house , a light which has a visible light source will have have a glare effect on your eyes. To prevent that we have to buy a LED panel light with an acrylic diffuser , which absorbs the glare and offers only illuminance to the room.
